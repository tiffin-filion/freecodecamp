Measure and Store Canister

Recycled wine bottles get a second life with these storage containers complete with nested measure tools.

An elegant alternative to plastic canisters, these handcrafted jars made from recycled wine bottles keep dry goods in sight and recipe-ready. Solid acacia wood measuring cups are nestled right atop each container, too, saving you prep time.

The containers feature glass pour spouts and either ⅓ and ½ cup measuring cups (large container) or 1 tbsp (medium container) nested in the lids. The larger measuring cup even has a ¼ line, making the all-in-one vessel a prime place to store your baking basics and go-to grains like quinoa, rice, or oatmeal. (Hello, even-easier overnight oats). Jars hand-finished in Colorado. Tops handmade in India.

made from
recycled glass, cork, acacia wood

measurements
Large canister 8" 40oz; wood top 6 1/8”H x 4 ¼”D Small canister 6" 32 oz; wood top 3 1/4"H x 4 1/4"D

care
Glass is microwave and dishwasher safe. Wood lids and cups should be hand washed; do not soak.

notes
Each glass jar and wood lid is handmade and may vary slightly in dimensions. Push cork in only far enough so that it is tight in the jar — do not try to force it in any further. There may be natural knots and slight color variation in the wood.
